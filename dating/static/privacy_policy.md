# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 2026*

## 1. Introduction

Hintder, operated by FOP Liubchenko Yurii Mykolaiovych ("we", "us", "our"), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. We act as the Data Controller for the personal data processed through our Service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI dating assistant.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Email address (via Google Sign-In or email link)
- Name and profile picture, if provided by your Google account
- Payment information (handled by our payment provider, not stored by us)

**Content You Upload:**
- Screenshots of dating profiles or chat threads
- Text you type (her replies, context, instructions)
- The voice/risk preferences and matches you save

Screenshots may contain personal data about other people (your match). You are responsible for ensuring you may share that content. We process it only to generate suggestions for you.

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

- Log data (IP address, browser/device information)
- Feature usage and interaction patterns
- Authentication tokens and preference cookies

### 2.3 Information From Third Parties

- Basic profile information from Google Sign-In
- Transaction status and payment confirmation from our payment provider

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Provide the Service:** create your account, read your screenshots into suggestions, coach replies, track date readiness, process subscriptions
- **Improve the Service:** analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop features
- **Communicate:** service notifications, support, and — with your consent — product updates
- **Security:** detect and prevent fraud and abuse, enforce our Terms

We do **not** use your uploaded screenshots or conversations to train AI models.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

- **Contract:** to provide the Service you requested
- **Consent:** for marketing communications
- **Legitimate Interests:** security, analytics, and service improvement
- **Legal Obligation:** to comply with applicable laws

## 5. Data Sharing

### 5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

### 5.2 Service Providers

We share data only with trusted providers who help us run the Service:
- **Google Cloud / Vertex AI (Gemini):** processes your screenshots and text to generate suggestions
- **Firebase (Google):** authentication
- **Google Cloud Storage:** temporary storage of uploaded screenshots
- **Paddle.com:** payments, acting as Merchant of Record

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, safety, or to investigate fraud.

### 5.4 Business Transfers

In a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may transfer to the new owner.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Uploaded screenshots:** kept for up to **30 days** so you can revisit a match, then automatically deleted by a storage lifecycle rule.
- **Saved matches (analysis, conversation, suggestions):** retained while your account is active.
- **Account data:** retained while your account is active and deleted within 30 days of account closure.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, export, or restrict processing of your personal data, to object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at **support@hintder.ai**.

## 8. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, role-based access control, secure authentication via Firebase, and continuous monitoring. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence (primarily on Google Cloud). We rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended only for adults aged **18 and over**. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 11. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and core functionality, and may use limited analytics cookies to understand usage. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically and will notify you of material changes via email, an in-app notice, or the updated date on this page.

## 13. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights:
