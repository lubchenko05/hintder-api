# Refund Policy

*Last updated: June 2026*

## Overview

Hintder operates on a subscription basis. We want you to be satisfied with the Service. This policy outlines our refund terms for all plans.

## 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a **14-day money-back guarantee** on all payments. If you are not satisfied with Hintder for any reason within 14 days of any payment, you may request a full refund — no questions asked.

To request a refund:
- Contact us at **support@hintder.ai** within 14 days of the payment
- This applies to all payments, including subscription renewals

## Monthly Subscriptions

You may request a full refund within 14 days of any monthly payment. After the 14-day window, monthly fees are non-refundable. You may cancel at any time — cancellation stops future renewals at the end of the current billing period, and the hints already on your balance remain yours.

## Annual Subscriptions

You may request a full refund within 14 days of any annual payment. Additionally, if you cancel within 30 days of an annual payment, you may request a prorated refund for the unused portion. After 30 days, no refunds are issued for annual subscriptions. You may cancel at any time.

## Hints

Hints are consumable credits. Subscription hints already spent are not separately refundable, but any payment is covered by the 14-day guarantee above. Cancelling does not erase the hints already on your balance.

## Additional Refund Circumstances

We may also issue refunds outside the 14-day guarantee in the following cases:

- **Billing Errors:** if you were charged incorrectly due to a technical error
- **Duplicate Charges:** if you were charged multiple times for the same plan
- **Extended Service Outage:** if the Service was unavailable for more than 72 consecutive hours due to issues on our end

## How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Pricing page. Cancellation stops future renewals; you keep access and hints through the period you've already paid for.

## Refund Requests

To request a refund, contact **support@hintder.ai** with:
- Your account email
- Transaction details
- The reason for the request

We review requests and respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds are processed through Paddle, our payment provider, and typically appear within 5–10 business days.

## Contact

Questions about this policy? Email **support@hintder.ai**.
